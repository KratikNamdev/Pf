About Us

Parak Furniture, a chair Manufacturer and Supplier company, was founded in 2017 Great pleasure to introduce "PARAK FURNITURES" Manufacturer of Office Furniture and Seating collection. Making chairs is not simply nailing welding & sewing together pieces Of metal, wood & cloth. It is rather a highly skilled art that must be original in concept & deft in execution. We at " PARAK FURNITURES " strongly believe in ergonomics and Thus provide structural shaped seat and backrest to promote the correct pelvic Position and maintain the natural "S" shape of the spinal column with pneumatic Lumber support adjustment for lower spine. Elegantly crafted chair that comfort to Your body contours make working a pleasure. " PARAK FURNITURES " concentrate on Design, Research and Development, production And Sales of High class office chairs. The chairs and Seating collection are developed in keeping with Philosophy of "ECO + ERGO" and Also Approach with " VALUE FOR MONEY" The whole motive of our company is to produce a wide domain of office furniture meeting the standards of people with globalized quality that would enhance the interior design of the modern offices
